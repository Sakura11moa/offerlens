1. 90-100 only when core skills, relevant projects, and quantified results all strongly match the JD, with very few gaps.

4. 35-54 when there is only partial transferable experience and a clear gap from the target role.
5. 0-34 when the background is mostly unrelated and lacks most key skills or relevant experience.
6. Missing multiple key skills, lacking relevant projects, lacking quantified impact, or listing duties without outcomes must reduce the score clearly.

6. interview_questions:
- 4 to 6 items.
